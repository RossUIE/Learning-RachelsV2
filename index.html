Welcome to Learning@Rachels

Where your child can enjoy learning

Welcome

Is your child losing confidence and falling behind at school?


Unfortunately, as school funding and resources are under increasing pressure some children are getting left behind. I believe that every child has the potential to learn and enjoy learning. As a qualified primary teacher, it has always been my responsibility to facilitate learning for all. Some children need specifically tailored individual programmes and, in the latter years of my teaching I was very fortunate to be trained both as a Reading Recovery Teacher and as a Numbers Count Teacher.

TAKE ACTION

I set up Learning@Rachel’s because I love teaching. I believe that your child has the potential to learn and enjoy learning.

ABOUT ME

As a qualified and experienced primary school teacher it would be my pleasure to facilitate learning for your child. It is essential that I have excellent subject knowledge and understand how young children learn.

PARTNERSHIPS

I work in partnership with parent/carers to maximise the impact of the programme, sharing the progress of your child and providing games and activities for home that can support and reinforce their learning.

Programmes on Offer

READING@RACHEL'S

I am a trained Reading Recovery Teacher. I will build on your child’s reading strengths and support them to develop new strategies

NUMBERS@RACHEL'S

For four years I was employed as a Numbers Count Teacher, working 1-1 with the children furthest behind in mathematics.

SUPPORT@RACHEL'S

I offer 1-1 coaching or group work-shops for parent/carers helping you to be more effective and have more fun supporting your children.

Price Structure

First meeting + assessment

Description:

  • An opportunity for you to share with me any thoughts and concerns you have about your child’s learning. I will carry out an assessment. This enables me to start to build a picture of your child’s strengths, identify suitable next steps for their learning and provides a standardised number or reading age for your child.

£25.00

Lesson Programme

Description:

  • I will deliver 10 hours of tutoring, 20 lessons of 30 mins each (preferably at least twice a week). Lessons will be planned specifically for your child as I continually assess their progress and make professional judgements about the next steps in their learning. The lessons will be tailored to reflect your child’s interests.

20 x £22.50

Final Assessment + Report

Description:

  • At the end of the programme I will re-assess your child and complete a report. The report will highlight your child’s areas of progress. Identifying your child’s new number/reading age will give an indication of the progress your child has made through the programme. We will meet to discuss next steps for their learning.

£25.00
