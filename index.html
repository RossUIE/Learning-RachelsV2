About Learning@Rachels

Is your child losing confidence and falling behind at school?


Unfortunately, as school funding and resources are under increasing pressure some children are getting left behind. I believe that every child has the potential to learn and enjoy learning. As a qualified primary teacher, it has always been my responsibility to facilitate learning for all. Some children need specifically tailored individual programmes and, in the latter years of my teaching I was very fortunate to be trained both as a Reading Recovery Teacher and as a Numbers Count Teacher.

Programmes on Offer

Some of the programmes currently available at Learning@Rachels

Reading@Rachel's

I am a trained Reading Recovery Teacher. I will build on your child’s reading strengths and support them to develop new strategies

Numbers@Rachel's

For four years I was employed as a Numbers Count Teacher, working 1-1 with the children furthest behind in mathematics.

Support@Rachel's

I offer 1-1 coaching or group work-shops for parent/carers helping you to be more effective and have more fun supporting your children

Pricing Structure

FIRST MEETING + ASSESSMENT

£ 25

Approx 1-2 Hours

  • An opportunity for you to share with me any thoughts and concerns you have about your child’s learning.
  • I will carry out an assessment
  • This assessment enables me to start to build a picture of your child’s strengths
  • Identify suitable next steps for their learning and provides a standardised number or reading age for your child.

LESSON PROGRAMME

£ 22.50
(x20)

Approx 10 Hours

  • I will deliver 10 hours of tutoring.
  • 20 lessons of 30 mins each (preferably at least twice a week).
  • Lessons will be planned specifically for your child.
  • All interior surfaces dressed and protected with the customers choice of finish (Natural, Satin or Gloss).
  • I continually assess their progress and make professional judgements about the next steps in their learning.
  • The lessons will be tailored to reflect your child’s interests.

FINAL ASSESSMENT + REPORT

£ 25

Approx 1 Hour

  • At the end of the programme I will re-assess your child and complete a report.
  • he report will highlight your child’s areas of progress.
  • Identifying your child’s new number/reading age will give an indication of the progress your child has made through the programme.
  • We will meet to discuss next steps for their learning.
